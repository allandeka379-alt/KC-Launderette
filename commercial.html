Capabilities

What we commit to in writing.

Volume capacity

Up to 800 kg per day at the current premises. Scaling to 1,200 kg with 30 days' notice through partner facilities.

Turnaround SLAs

24-hour standard for hotel and restaurant linens. 12-hour express on agreed daily windows. Penalty clauses on missed SLAs.

Logistics

Scheduled daily or thrice-weekly collection. Trolley exchange, sealed laundry bags with property tags, signed handover sheets.

Inventory tracking

Per-property tagging on linens. Monthly reconciliation of items in, items out, and items in repair. Loss rate published quarterly.

Invoicing & terms

Monthly invoicing in USD or ZWG. Net-14 terms by default, with longer terms available on review. Itemised statements per pickup.

Insurance

Goods-in-transit and process cover up to USD 50,000 per incident. Certificates available on request before contract sign-off.
