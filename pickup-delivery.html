Windows & fees

Practical details, in plain English.

Pickup windows

Mon–Sat

8am–6pm. Two-hour windows on the day of pickup. Sundays by arrangement (+$5 fee).

Standard fee

Free

Over $20 per order, both ways. Otherwise $3 flat — same fee whether the order is one shirt or twenty.

Turnaround

48 hrs

From pickup to return on standard wash & fold and dry cleaning. Express adds 50% for 24-hour return.

The process

Five steps. No paperwork.

  1. 01

    Message us with your suburb and an idea of the load.

    Photos are welcome. We confirm pricing and propose a pickup window within minutes during business hours.

  2. 02

    A driver collects on your chosen window.

    Two-hour window, with a 15-minute heads-up message before arrival. Items are weighed or counted on collection.

  3. 03

    We process at the premises.

    Sorted, washed, dried, pressed and inspected. Anything unexpected — a tag missing, a stain that won't shift — and we message you before proceeding.

  4. 04

    Quality check & ready notice.

    Once everything passes inspection, we message that the order is ready and confirm a delivery window with you.

  5. 05

    Delivered to your door.

    Folded items are wrapped, hung items are bagged. Payment on delivery — cash, mobile money, or bank transfer.
